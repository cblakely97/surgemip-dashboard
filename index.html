About

STOFS2D-Global SurgeMIP runs multi-decade storm surge simulations on a global unstructured mesh using ADCIRC. Three scenarios are in progress:

  • CFS/CFSv2 Reanalysis (1979–2024) — Historical hindcast driven by NCEP CFS and CFSv2 atmospheric reanalysis
  • HadGEM3-GC31-HM Present (1979–2014) — CMIP6 HighResMIP present-climate simulation
  • HadGEM3-GC31-HM Future (2015–2050) — CMIP6 HighResMIP future-climate projection
